About Me

I am a passionate Health Data Analyst with a background in Health Informatics and Data Analytics. My mission is to leverage data to improve healthcare outcomes and enhance clinical decision-making.

I am skilled in Python, SQL, R, and Tableau, and have worked on various projects involving predictive modeling, healthcare data analysis, and AI-driven interventions. My diverse experience spans across the healthcare and technology domains, making me well-equipped to handle complex data challenges in healthcare.

Key Projects

Predictive Modeling on Excess Readmission Ratio

Utilized data from MIMIC-III to build a model that predicts readmission risks for patients with heart diseases. This project involved data mining, predictive modeling, and building dashboards in Tableau for easy visualization of the readmission risks.

AI-Assisted Antidepressant Efficacy Prediction

Developed a predictive model using data from the All of Us research program to predict antidepressant efficacy in African American populations. The project involved the use of machine learning techniques and statistical analysis.

Fluoride Content & Fluorosis Risk Assessment

Analyzed the fluoride content in water and its association with fluorosis risks. The project utilized data visualization tools and provided actionable insights into public health and policy.
